Why it works

Serious outcomes, disguised as an unforgettable day.

Bowling and escape rooms entertain a team. They rarely change one. We pair the wisdom of craft with proven coaching and project-management methodologies, so the fun carries real, discussable results back into your organisation.

No. 1

Collaboration

Making one product with many hands exposes how your team plans, sequences and shares ownership, and gives them a shared success to point to.

No. 2

Communication

Craft makes the gap between what is said and what is understood physically visible, then our facilitation helps the team close it.

No. 3

Human connection

Phones off, hands busy, hierarchy flattened at the workbench. People meet each other again, as people, not job titles.

No. 4

Intercultural competence

Working alongside Tunisian master artisans is a live exercise in listening, respect and collaborating across languages and cultures.

Signature experiences

Every craft teaches a different lesson.

A selection of our formats, each pairing one craft with one team dynamic. Your program is composed from these and tailored to your goals.

Collaboration

The Loom: Team Weaving

One carpet, one team. Every thread depends on the one before it: roles, rhythm and hand-overs become tangible as your team weaves a single piece together, and keeps it.

Communication

Back to Back: Ceramics in Pairs

Sitting back to back, one partner guides only with words; the other shapes the clay from what they hear. The distance between instruction and interpretation has never been this visible, or this discussable.

Problem-solving

Beautiful Mistakes: Calligraphy

Pairs start each other’s pieces with a deliberate “wrong” stroke, then finish them beautifully. A masterclass in reframing errors, building on others’ work and finding solutions instead of blame.

Shared vision

The Mosaic: Team Collage

Each person shapes their own fragment; only together do the pieces form one picture. A lasting artwork for your office, and a lasting metaphor for how your team fits together.
