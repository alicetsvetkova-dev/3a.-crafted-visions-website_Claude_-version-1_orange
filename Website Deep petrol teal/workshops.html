Individual Workshops · Tunis

Slow down. Make something that lasts.

Weekly small-group sessions with master artisans: ceramics, Arabic calligraphy, bookbinding and carpet weaving. No experience needed. You leave with a piece made by your own hands.

Choose your date Explore the crafts
The crafts

Four crafts. Four master artisans. One price.

Every session is hands-on from the first minute, capped at six participants, and led by an artisan who has practiced their craft for a lifetime. 49 € per person, everything included.

Traditional Ceramics

Wednesdays & Saturdays · 10:00 – 13:00

Shape clay the way it has been shaped here for centuries. Slow, tactile and deeply grounding.

Arabic Calligraphy

Fridays, Saturdays & Sundays · 10:00 – 13:00

Stroke by stroke, a meditative practice of precision, rhythm and meaning with a master calligrapher.

Bookbinding

Thursdays, Fridays & Saturdays · 15:00 – 18:00

Fold, stitch and press your own book with one of the last traditional bookbinders in Tunis.

Carpet Weaving

Sundays · 10:00 – 14:00

Work a traditional loom side by side with a weaver: wool, knots, pattern and patience.
Book your workshop

Choose a date. We’ll take it from there.

Marked dates have open sessions, bookable up to six months ahead. Pick a day, open a session for the details, and complete your booking through secure Stripe checkout. Confirmation within 24 hours.

Good to know

The practical part.

Pick a date in the calendar and pay securely via Stripe. You’ll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours with the exact meeting point in Tunis and everything you need to know. Sessions run from a minimum of 2 up to a maximum of 6 participants. If the minimum isn’t reached, you choose between a full refund or another date.

None at all. Every workshop is designed for complete beginners and guided step by step by a master artisan. All materials, snacks and drinks are included. You only bring yourself.

Workshops run in English and French. The artisans primarily speak French and Arabic; we make sure nothing gets lost. Part of the magic is discovering how much craft can teach without words.

Free cancellation up to 7 days before your workshop: full refund, no questions asked. Within 7 days of the session, bookings are non-refundable, but you may transfer your spot to someone else.
