Hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., USA). When you visit, your browser transmits technical data (including your IP address, browser type and time of access) to the host, which processes it in server log files to deliver the site securely. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in operating a stable, secure website (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Pages load the “Spectral” and “Hanken Grotesk” typefaces from Google Fonts (Google Ireland Ltd.). When a font is requested, your IP address is transmitted to Google. [Recommended: self-host the fonts to avoid this transfer, ask your developer.]

Contact & inquiry forms

When you use the contact or team-building inquiry form, the data you enter (such as name, email, company, and your message) is transmitted through our forms processor [Formspree, Inc., USA, confirm] and delivered to us by email. We use it solely to respond to your request. Legal basis: steps prior to entering a contract and our legitimate interest in answering enquiries (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR). Newsletter

If you subscribe, your email address is processed by our newsletter provider Mailchimp (Intuit Inc., USA) to send you our updates. Subscription uses a confirmation (double opt-in) step; you can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email. Legal basis: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

Bookings & payment

Workshop bookings are completed through Stripe (Stripe, Inc. / Stripe Payments Europe Ltd.). Payment and billing details are entered on Stripe’s checkout and processed by Stripe as an independent controller/processor under its own privacy policy; we do not receive or store your full card details. Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Links to social platforms

Our site links to Instagram and WhatsApp. We do not embed their tracking on our pages; their data practices apply only once you follow a link and interact with those services.
